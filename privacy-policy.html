Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 22, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Today in History ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Chrome extension.

Information We Collect

Today in History is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information to provide you with historical data:

  • No Personal Information: We do not collect any personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or contact details.
  • Date Preferences: The extension locally stores your preferred date selections and theme settings on your device only.
  • Usage Data: We do not track your browsing habits or collect analytics data.

Data Sources

Historical data is retrieved from public APIs:

  • History API: We use history.muffinlabs.com to fetch historical events, births, and deaths.
  • Wikipedia Links: Event details link to Wikipedia articles for additional information.

Data Storage

All your preferences and settings are stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. This includes:

  • Theme preference (light/dark mode)
  • View mode preference (normal/compact)
  • Last selected date

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

Permissions

Our extension requests minimal permissions:

  • Storage: To save your preferences locally on your device
  • Host Permissions: To fetch historical data from history.muffinlabs.com

Third-Party Services

We use the following external services:

  • history.muffinlabs.com: Provides historical event data. Please refer to their privacy policy for information about their data practices.
  • Wikipedia: Event links redirect to Wikipedia for detailed information. Wikipedia has its own privacy policy.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • HTTPS is used for all external API communications
  • No user data is transmitted to our servers

Children's Privacy

Our extension does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has used our extension, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal information, there's no personal data to access, modify, or delete. You can:

  • Clear your preferences by removing and reinstalling the extension
  • Disable the extension at any time through Chrome's extension settings
  • Uninstall the extension completely if you no longer wish to use it

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our practices, please contact us:

  • Email: support@thebesttools.net
  • Web Store: Chrome Web Store Page

We will respond to your inquiry within 48 hours.
